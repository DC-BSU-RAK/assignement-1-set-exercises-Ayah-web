Terraria

Terraria is a 2D adventure game where you can explore, build, and fight in a randomly created world ranging from normal to large.
Think of it this way, Minecraft meets 2D gaming! Dig underground to find treasure (sometimes guided by fairies),
craft cool weapons and tools, and take on big bosses like the Eye of Cthulhu. Build your own base, meet fun characters,
and survive crazy events like goblin invasions. With millions of items to collect and secrets to discover, Terraria
is absolutely packed with fun. Whether you like building, fighting, or exploring, this game will definitely be up your ally!


Characters (NPCs)


Pre-Hardmode NPCs

  • Guide - Provides crafting recipes and tips for beginners.
  • Merchant - Sells basic items like ropes, torches, and piggy banks.
  • Nurse - Heals the player for a fee.
  • Demolitionist - Sells explosives and grenades.
  • Dryad - Sells nature-related items and provides world status information.
  • Arms Dealer - Sells guns and ammunition.
  • Tavernkeep - Sells items related to the Old One's Army event.
  • Stylist - Changes the player's hairstyle and sells hair dyes.
  • Goblin Tinkerer - Sells the Tinkerer's Workshop and offers reforge services.
  • Wizard - Sells magical items like spells and mana potions.
  • Clothier - Sells vanity items and can summon Skeletron at night.
  • Mechanic - Sells wires, switches, and other mechanical items.
  • Painter - Sells paint and painting tools.
  • Dye Trader - Sells dyes and exchanges Strange Plants for them.
  • Angler - Gives fishing quests and rewards.
  • Party Girl - Sells fun and festive items like bubble machines.
  • Witch Doctor - Sells summoner-related items and furniture.
  • Pirate - Sells pirate-themed items and cannons.
  • Truffle - Sells mushroom-themed items and requires a surface Glowing Mushroom biome.
  • Zoologist - Sells animal-related items and unlocks as you complete the Bestiary.
  • Golfer - Sells golf-related items and unlocks after finding him underground.

Hardmode NPCs

  • Steampunker - Sells the Clentaminator and other steampunk-themed items.
  • Santa Claus - Sells Christmas-themed items during the holiday season.
  • Princess - Sells decorative items and spawns after all other NPCs are present.
  • Cyborg - Sells rockets and high-tech items.

Special NPCs

  • Traveling Merchant - Appears randomly and sells rare items.
  • Skeleton Merchant - Found underground and sells unique items like the Magic Lantern.
  • Town Pets - Non-functional NPCs that act as pets for your town (ex: Bunny, Cat, Dog).
  • Old Man - Summons Skeletron at the Dungeon entrance.

Biomes Found


Surface-level Biomes

  • Forest - The default biome with basic enemies, trees, and minimal hazards.
  • Desert - A sandy biome with cacti, Antlions, and occasional pyramids.
  • Snow (Tundra) - A cold biome with ice blocks, snow, and icy enemies.
  • Jungle - A dense, tropical biome with mud, water, and dangerous enemies like Hornets.
  • Ocean - Located at the far ends of the world, featuring water, sharks, and coral.
  • Mushroom - A glowing mushroom-themed biome that can be created artificially or found underground.
  • Hallow - A magical, pastel-colored biome that spawns after defeating the Wall of Flesh.
  • Corruption - A dark, evil biome with chasms, Shadow Orbs, and dangerous enemies.
  • Crimson - An alternate evil biome with gory themes, Crimson Hearts, and unique enemies.
  • Dungeon - A dangerous, pre-Hardmode biome guarded by Skeletron, filled with traps and loot.
  • Graveyard - A spooky biome created by placing too many Tombstones, with unique crafting stations.

Underground Biomes

  • Underground Forest - A subterranean version of the Forest biome with basic enemies and resources.
  • Underground Desert - A sandy underground biome with Antlions, traps, and unique loot.
  • Underground Snow (Ice Cavern) - A chilly biome with ice-themed enemies and frozen chests.
  • Underground Jungle - A dense, dangerous biome with Bee Hives, Temple entrances, and powerful enemies.
  • Underground Mushroom - A glowing mushroom biome found deep underground, home to Truffle Worms.
  • Underground Hallow - The underground version of the Hallow biome with crystal shards and magical enemies.
  • Underground Corruption - The underground version of the Corruption biome with Demon Altars and tougher enemies.
  • Underground Crimson - The underground version of the Crimson biome with Crimstone and dangerous foes.
  • Cavern Layer - A general underground biome with ore deposits, water, and common enemies.
  • Glowing Mushroom Cavern - A rare, naturally occurring biome with glowing mushrooms and unique enemies.

Hardmode Biomes

  • Corrupted Desert - A Desert biome overtaken by the Corruption.
  • Crimson Desert - A Desert biome overtaken by the Crimson.
  • Hallow Desert - A Desert biome overtaken by the Hallow.
  • Corrupted Ice - A Snow biome overtaken by the Corruption.
  • Crimson Ice - A Snow biome overtaken by the Crimson.
  • Hallow Ice - A Snow biome overtaken by the Hallow.
  • Corrupted Jungle - A Jungle biome overtaken by the Corruption.
  • Crimson Jungle - A Jungle biome overtaken by the Crimson.
  • Hallow Jungle - A Jungle biome overtaken by the Hallow.

Mini Biomes

  • Oasis - A small, lush area found in the Desert biome with unique enemies and loot.
  • Enchanted Sword Shrine - A rare mini-biome with a stone background and a chance to find an Enchanted Sword.
  • Gem Cave - A small cave filled with gemstones.
  • Floating Lake - A small body of water found in the Cavern Layer.
  • Mushroom Grove - A small area with glowing mushrooms and glowing mushroom trees.
  • Spider Nest - A mini-biome filled with cobwebs and Black Recluse spiders.

Special Biomes

  • Underworld (Hell) - A fiery biome at the bottom of the world, filled with lava, demons, and Hellstone.
  • Sky Islands (Heaven) - Floating islands in the sky with houses, loot, and Harpy enemies.
  • Space - The top layer of the world with low gravity and Harpies.
  • Temple - A Hardmode biome found in the Underground Jungle, filled with traps and the Golem boss.
  • Aether - A shimmering biome found underground, featuring the Shimmer liquid and unique mechanics.

Event Biomes

  • Meteorite - Created when a meteor crashes, featuring Meteorite ore and Meteor Heads.
  • Bee Hive - Found in the Underground Jungle, containing honey and the Queen Bee boss.
  • Plantera's Bulb - A glowing bulb found in the Underground Jungle, used to summon Plantera.
  • Lihzahrd Temple - A Hardmode biome with Lihzahrds, traps, and the Golem boss.
  • Marble Cave - A biome with marble blocks and Medusa enemies.
  • Granite Cave - A biome with granite blocks and Granite Elementals.
  • Spider Cave - A biome filled with cobwebs and Black Recluse spiders.
