Who Might You Meet ?

Pre-Hardmode NPCs

  • Guide - Provides crafting recipes and tips for beginners.
  • Merchant - Sells basic items like ropes, torches, and piggy banks.
  • Nurse - Heals the player for a fee.
  • Demolitionist - Sells explosives and grenades.
  • Dryad - Sells nature-related items and provides world status information.
  • Arms Dealer - Sells guns and ammunition.
  • Tavernkeep - Sells items related to the Old One's Army event.
  • Stylist - Changes the player's hairstyle and sells hair dyes.
  • Goblin Tinkerer - Sells the Tinkerer's Workshop and offers reforge services.
  • Wizard - Sells magical items like spells and mana potions.
  • Clothier - Sells vanity items and can summon Skeletron at night.
  • Mechanic - Sells wires, switches, and other mechanical items.
  • Painter - Sells paint and painting tools.
  • Dye Trader - Sells dyes and exchanges Strange Plants for them.
  • Angler - Gives fishing quests and rewards.
  • Party Girl - Sells fun and festive items like bubble machines.
  • Witch Doctor - Sells summoner-related items and furniture.
  • Pirate - Sells pirate-themed items and cannons.
  • Truffle - Sells mushroom-themed items and requires a surface Glowing Mushroom biome.
  • Zoologist - Sells animal-related items and unlocks as you complete the Bestiary.
  • Golfer - Sells golf-related items and unlocks after finding him underground.

Hardmode NPCs

  • Steampunker - Sells the Clentaminator and other steampunk-themed items.
  • Santa Claus - Sells Christmas-themed items during the holiday season.
  • Princess - Sells decorative items and spawns after all other NPCs are present.
  • Cyborg - Sells rockets and high-tech items.

Special NPCs

  • Traveling Merchant - Appears randomly and sells rare items.
  • Skeleton Merchant - Found underground and sells unique items like the Magic Lantern.
  • Town Pets - Non-functional NPCs that act as pets for your town (ex: Bunny, Cat, Dog).
  • Old Man - Summons Skeletron at the Dungeon entrance.
