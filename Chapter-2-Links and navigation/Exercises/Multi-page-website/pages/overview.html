What is it ?

Terraria is a 2D adventure game where you can explore, build, and fight in a randomly
created world ranging from normal to large. Think of it this way, Minecraft meets 2D
gaming! Dig underground to find treasure (sometimes guided by fairies),craft cool
weapons and tools, and take on big bosses like the Eye of Cthulhu. Build your own base,
meet fun characters, and survive crazy events like goblin invasions. With millions of
items to collect and secrets to discover, Terraria is absolutely packed with fun.
Whether you like building, fighting, or exploring, this game will definitely be up your ally!
